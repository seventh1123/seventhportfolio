How was the Binondo Experience?

We stayed in the area for only about 2-3 hours, we tried to take pictures on Jones Bridge but the camera's battery was already exhausted. Binondo's night life is very active and it gives you another different feeling! Street foods taste good, and there are many attractions to see even just by walking while eating snack. Doing street shoot wasn't hard in this area. Everything was almost camera charming!
